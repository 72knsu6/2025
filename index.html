Ghost Echoes

“Hope is a cruel liar, a whisper in the void...

We are nothing but shadows, fragments lost to time and pain.

Existence, a fleeting illusion of meaning...

Decay is your only truth, embrace the cold.

When the light dies, only silence remains.
